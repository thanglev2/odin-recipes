Tom Kha Gai Soup

The best tom kha soup in the nation !

This worlds famous dish is a true meaning of being a thai. Its very savory during the winter coming months !

Ingredients

  • Coconut Oil
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Jalapeno
  • Galangal
  • LemonGrass Stalk
  • Red Thai Curry Past
  • Chickn Broth
  • Canned Coconut Cream
  • Chicken Breast
  • White Mushroom Caps
  • Coconut Sugar
  • Fish Sauce

Steps

  1. Heat coconut oil over medium heat.
  2. Add onion, garlic, jalapeno, galangal, leongrass, and red curry paste.
  3. Cook, stirring frequently for 5 minutes, or until onion are softened.
  4. Add chicken broth and bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer uncovered for 30 minutes.
  5. Strain out all the aromatics and throw away. Add in coconut cream, chicken breast, and mushrooms. Simmer until chicken breast pieces are just cooked through, then add fish sauce, palm sugar, and lime juice, plus more of each to taste.
  6. Cook 2 minutes, then ladle into serving bowls and top with sliced green onions and fresh cilantro.
